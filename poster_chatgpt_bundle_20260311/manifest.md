# Poster Review Bundle

This bundle is meant to be attached to the ChatGPT 5.4 Pro web interface for a
wholesale redesign of the CS234 poster by modifying `generate_poster.py`.

Interpretation note:
- Treat the bundled files as the source of truth for substantive project
  content, available assets, real metrics, and course requirements.
- Do not treat the current visual formatting of `generate_presentation.py` or
  the generated slide frames as authoritative. They are content references, and
  their layout may be improved aggressively in the poster redesign.

## Included files

- `qanta-buzzer/generate_poster.py`
  - Current poster generator to be improved.
- `qanta-buzzer/generated/quizbowl_mc_stopping_data_driven/poster.pdf`
  - Current generated poster PDF.
- `qanta-buzzer/generated/quizbowl_mc_stopping_data_driven/poster.png`
  - Current generated poster preview.
- `qanta-buzzer/generated/quizbowl_mc_stopping_data_driven/`
  - Full generated presentation/poster asset directory, including frames, GIF,
    contact sheet, poster PDF, and poster PNG.
- `qanta-buzzer/generate_presentation.py`
  - Presentation generator whose frames are reused by the poster.
- `qanta-buzzer/artifacts/smoke/evaluation_report.json`
  - Smoke evaluation metrics used by the poster; do not invent numbers that
    conflict with this file.
- `ed_threads.md`
  - Local CS234 Ed archive, including poster guidance threads.
- `ed_threads.json`
  - Same Ed archive in JSON form.
- `poster_guidelines/google_doc_poster_guidelines.txt`
  - Text export of the Google Doc poster/video guidelines.
- `poster_guidelines/source_summary.md`
  - Short summary of the specific poster-related guidance extracted from the
    course sources.
- `chatgpt54_pro_prompt.txt`
  - Paste-ready prompt for ChatGPT 5.4 Pro.
